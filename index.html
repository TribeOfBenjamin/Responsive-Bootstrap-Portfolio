About

Hello there!

My name is Benjamin Vasko. I work as an assistant principal at a private elementary and middle school in the Twin Cities. I also teach geometry and Latin to the middle school. I graduated with baccealuerate degrees in philosophy and Catholic studies from the University of Saint Thomas, where I also played on the men's lacrosse team.

In 2018, a friend of mine and I founded a software company called Schedule My Teachers, which helps school administrators create their school's academic schedule. In hopes of helping my co-founder with the programming, I decided to learn computer programming myself. I am currently enrolled in the University of Minnesota's Coding Bootcamp and will finish the program in September of this year. In my free time I like to play sports, bike, canoe, and camp. SKOL.
